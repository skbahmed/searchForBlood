instructions for donor

  • ✓ Get plenty of sleep the night before you plan to donate.
  • ✓ Eat a healthy meal before your donation.
  • ✓ Avoid fatty foods, such as hamburgers, french fries or ice cream before donating.
  • ✓ Drink an extra 16 ounces (473 ml) of water and other fluids before the donation.
  • ✓ A platelet donor must not take aspirin for two days prior to donating.

Benefits of Blood Donation

  • ✓ Reduce harmful iron stores.
  • ✓ Preserve cardiovascular health.
  • ✓ Reduce the risk cancer.
  • ✓ Burn calories.
  • ✓ Give you a sense of pride
  • ✓ Free blood analysis, include : hepatitis B, hepatitis C, HIV, West Nile virus, Trypanosoma cruzi, syphilis.

Who Can Donate Blood?

  • Age : At least 17 years old.
  • High Blood Pressure : Below 180 systolic and below 100 diastolic at the time of donation.
  • Body Piercing : Have not a tongue, nose, belly button or genital piercing in the past 12 months.
  • Cold and Flu : After completed antibiotic treatment.
  • MSM : Men who have had sex with other men can not donate.
  • Tattoos : One-year deferral.
  • Weight : Weight at least 110 pounds or 50 kg.
